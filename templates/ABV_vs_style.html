ABV vs. Style

In the beginning of our analysis, we wanted to find what was common among certain styles, and analyzed the ABV of each style, by everaging the ABV of each style of beer.

We then narrowed it down to the top 8 styles with the highest ABV.


We then compared the top styles produced by demand to find what the if the general population prefers a higher, or lower ABV.
